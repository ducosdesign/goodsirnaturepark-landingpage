Join Us for a Cause

Canadian Botanical Conservatory

Goodsir Nature Park is a beloved community treasure in Salmon Valley, B.C., providing residents with beautiful trails, natural habitats, and peaceful recreation spaces. Our park serves as a vital green sanctuary where families gather, children explore, and wildlife thrives.

As stewards of this precious natural resource, we are committed to preserving and enhancing the park for future generations. The funds raised through this community potluck will directly support essential maintenance, conservation efforts, and accessibility improvements.

What Your Support Provides

  • Trail Maintenance: Keep paths safe, clear, and accessible year-round for all visitors
  • Wildlife Conservation: Protect native species and restore critical natural habitats
  • Educational Programs: Guided nature walks, workshops, and environmental education for all ages
  • Community Spaces: Enhanced picnic areas and gathering spots for families and groups
  • Accessibility Projects: Creating barrier-free pathways to ensure nature is available to everyone
  • Interpretive Signage: New educational displays to help visitors learn about local flora and fauna

By attending our potluck fundraiser, you're not just enjoying a wonderful community meal - you're investing in the health and sustainability of our shared natural spaces. Every contribution, whether through attendance, donations, or volunteer time, makes a meaningful difference in preserving this treasured park.
